Phillippines Style Spaghetti

Filipino sweet-style spaghetti is a unique and popular dish in the Philippines that puts a sweet twist on the traditional Italian spaghetti. It is characterized by its sweet, thick, and flavorful tomato-based sauce, which sets it apart from the savory and tangy Italian version. The sauce is typically made with tomato sauce or ketchup, brown sugar, banana ketchup (a sweet and tangy condiment), and sometimes condensed milk or evaporated milk, which contributes to its distinct sweetness.

Filipino sweet-style spaghetti often includes additional ingredients such as ground meat (usually pork or beef), sliced hot dogs or sausage, onions, and garlic. The sauce is usually simmered for a longer period to allow the flavors to meld together, resulting in a rich and sweet taste. The dish is commonly served with cooked spaghetti noodles and garnished with grated cheese on top, adding a salty and creamy element to balance the sweetness.

Steps in coooking Spaghetti

  1. Cooking The Noodles

    Boiling water in your cooking pot, and then sprinkle in just a teaspoon of salt. Add your spaghetti noodles inside, and just cook them as indicated in the package.

  2. Creating The Sauce And Cooking The Meat

    Add cooking oil in your pot, and then adding your garlic and onion. Sauté this until the onion gets soft. First incorporate the ground pork, and cook this up while stirring. You can also add your ham and hotdogs once the pork has gotten light brown. Sauté everything together for 2 minutes.

  3. Adding The Veggies And Spaghetti Sauce

    Cooking the bell peppers, add these ingredients into the pot, and then just let this cook for 1 minute. Pouring the spaghetti sauce inside. Once you have integrated this, you can also put 1 cup of water inside. Stir the ingredients together, and wait for the mixture to boil.

  4. Placing Some Final Seasonings In The Sauce

    Incorporating Knorr Pork Cube, after you put the cube inside, you should place the cover on top. Take the heat down a bit so it can simmer. And then keep everything cooking for 30 minutes. But every once in a while, specifically after every 6 minutes, you should stir the mixture.Adding salt and ground black pepper. Place as much as you would like for this. There is also the option of grating some cheese on top if you want some added cheesiness from the get go.

  5. Plating The Spaghetti

    Placing the spaghetti noodles on a plate. Make sure to arrange this as neatly as you can. Then top it off with your spaghetti sauce from earlier. Also add your shredded cheese as a topping.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. spaghetti noodles
  • 1 lb. ground pork
  • 35 ounces Filipino style spaghetti sauce
  • 1 Knorr Pork Cube
  • 3 ounces ham sliced
  • 5 hotdogs sliced diagonally
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper minced
  • 1/2 cup green bell pepper minced
  • 1 yellow onion minced
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
